Birthday Party Supplies:

  • Balloons - Various colors for decoration.

  • Cake - Choose a flavor everyone will enjoy.

  • Invitation - Send out digital or printed invites.

  • Party favors - Small gifts for guests to take home.

  • Decoration - Streamers, banners, and themed items.

Party Schedule:

  1. Setup - Decorate and prepare food from 4:00 PM.

  2. Guest Arrival - Welcome guests starting at 5:00 PM.

  3. Games - Start with fun activities at 5:30 PM.

  4. Cake Cutting - Celebrate with cake at 6:30 PM.

  5. Dinner - Serve food around 7:00 PM.

  6. Gift Opening - Open presents at 7:45 PM.

  7. Dance/Music - Turn on the music, let's dance at 8:15 PM.

  8. Goodbye - Wrap up the party by 9:00 PM.

Party Roles and Responsibilities:

Host
>>Responsible for setting up all decorations and creating the party atmosphere.

Decorator
>>Responsible for setting up all Decorations and creating the party atmosphere.

Chef
>>Prepare or coordinates food, including the birthday cake.

Entertainment Director
>>Organizes games, music, and activities for guests.

Cleanup Crew
>>Handles tidying up before, during, and after the party.
